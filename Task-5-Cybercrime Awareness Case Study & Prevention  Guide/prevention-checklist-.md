# &#x20;               **Stay Safe Online' checklist**



\---



**✅ Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)**



**🔐 General Safety Tips**



✔️ Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA)



✔️ Verify links before clicking—avoid unknown or shortened URLs



✔️ Keep your apps and devices updated to prevent security vulnerabilities



✔️ Do not share personal information (OTP, passwords, ID details)



✔️ Log out from public computers and avoid saving passwords



✔️ Be cautious of fake job offers or internship scams



**➕ Additional General Tips**



✔️ Review app permissions and avoid giving unnecessary access (camera, contacts, location)



✔️ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking



✔️ Enable screen lock (PIN fingerprint) on your phone and laptop



✔️ Backup important data regularly to prevent loss from cyber attacks



✔️ Follow only verified accounts for official updates and information



\---



**💳 UPI \& Financial Safety Tips**



✔️ Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone



✔️ Always check the receiver’s name before payment



✔️ Avoid scanning unknown QR codes



✔️ Do not approve unknown “collect requests”



**➕ Additional Financial UPI Tips**



✔️ Set a daily transaction limit on your banking UPI apps



✔️ Enable instant SMS email alerts for every transaction



✔️ Use only official apps like bank apps or trusted UPI apps (avoid third-party APKs)



✔️ Double-check payment requests received on WhatsApp or social media



✔️ Immediately block your account if you notice suspicious activity
