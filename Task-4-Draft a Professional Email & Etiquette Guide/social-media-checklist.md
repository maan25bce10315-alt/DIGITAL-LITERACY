# Responsible Social Media Use -- Checklist for College Students

## ✅ Do's

-   Think before you post -- ensure your content reflects your values
    and future goals\
-   Keep your profiles professional, especially on platforms like
    LinkedIn\
-   Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation\
-   Respect others' opinions and engage in healthy discussions\
-   Use privacy settings wisely to control who sees your content\
-   Share achievements and skills to build a positive digital identity

## ❌ Don'ts

-   Don't post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content\
-   Don't overshare personal details like address, phone number, or
    location\
-   Don't engage in online arguments or trolling\
-   Don't blindly follow trends that may harm your reputation\
-   Don't ignore your digital footprint---what you post stays online\
-   Don't use social media excessively at the cost of studies or health
